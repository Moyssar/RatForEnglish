Rat for English

Its a platform concerned with developing, practicing and teaching the English language in a modern and fun way and creating a stimulating environment for everyone interested in the English language.

about us

origin :

The platform was launched in Shendi in November 2023.

effectiveness :

I ran a reading challenge competition and a best presentation competition. The platform has cooperated with a number of centers and institutes, including:

  • British Education Centers
  • Shendi Youth Development and Training Center

And it continues to provide programs and give an opportunity to everyone interested in the English language.
